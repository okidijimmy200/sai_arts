Aramu Bito

Aramu Bito is a Ugandan creative writer, a scholar and a recording underground rapper based in Gulu, Custom Corner whose works explores African culture, identity, history, philosophy, religion, politics, sociology, anthropology, education, economy and problems among others. Aramu Bito is also the founder of Boo-Kec Rap music which specifically works on songs that explore and expose the causes, effects and solutions to war, domestic violence, child abuse, crimes, drug abuse, poverty, prostitution, corruption and trauma in the now post war region of northern Uganda with an aim of restoring mental health, peace, prosperity, unity, love, positive attitude and freedom among the youth for a better social transformation. He is also a teacher of literature in English and both English and Chinese language with fluency and ability in public speaking with the vision of empowering the post war traumatized youth with attitude and skills that can make them achieve their dreams.
